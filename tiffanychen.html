About Me

My name is Xing Chen. I am 16 years old and a rising senior in Fiorello H. Laguardia High School for the Performing Arts majoring in the violin.

Even though I attend an arts school, I am interested in academics. My favorite subject in school is AP Environmental Science. I am passionate about sustainability and preventing climate change.

After a year in this class, I have decided to pursue this interest and continue to learn about the injustices in society regarding the environment. I truly believe that climate change is currently a major problem and people are not aware of the changes it has already made in our lives.

Being in Laguardia High School has allowed me to follow my interests in academics and music. It has educated me and enabled me to handle multiple tasks at once.
